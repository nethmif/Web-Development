Sigiriya

Sigiriya, also identified as the Lion’s rock, is a rock fortress and a castle found in the Matale district of Sri Lanka. Some of the most attractive parts of Sigiriya are its gardens, Lion’s Paws, frescos, mirror walls, and many more. The complex hydraulics system at the fortress enhances the site’s reputation as a beautiful example of classical city planning.

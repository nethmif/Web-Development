Sigiriya


Sigiriya Tour Plan


$800.00

Sigiriya and Beyond: Culture in Sri Lanka


Day 1: Arrival in Sigiriya


  1. Arrive in Sigiriya and check into your hotel.
  2. Rest and relax for the day after your journey.

Day 2: Explore Sigiriya Rock Fortress


  1. Head to Sigiriya Rock Fortress early in the morning to beat the crowds and the heat.
  2. Climb up to the top of the fortress to see the stunning views and learn about the history and culture of the area.
  3. Visit the Sigiriya Museum to see the artifacts and displays related to the fortress.
  4. In the evening, you can take a leisurely stroll around the Sigiriya village and see the local way of life.

Day 3: Visit nearby attractions


  1. After breakfast, visit the nearby Dambulla Cave Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its beautiful Buddhist cave paintings and sculptures.
  2. After that, head to the Minneriya National Park for a wildlife safari and enjoy the beautiful scenery and wildlife of the park.
  3. Return to your hotel in the evening and relax.

Day 4: Explore Anuradhapura


  1. Head to Anuradhapura, one of the ancient capital cities of Sri Lanka and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  2. Visit the sacred Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi tree, believed to have been grown from a branch of the Bodhi tree under which Buddha attained enlightenment.
  3. Explore the ancient ruins of the city, including the magnificent stupas, palaces, and temples.
  4. Return to your hotel in Sigiriya in the evening.

Day 5: Departure from Sigiriya


  1. After breakfast, check out from your hotel and depart for your onward journey.

Nilaweli


Nilaweli Tour Plan


$700.00

Leisurely Escape to Sandy Shores: A Beach and Wildlife Adventure in Nilaweli, Sri Lanka


Day 1: Arrival in Colombo


  1. Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport in Colombo and transfer to your hotel in Nilaweli Beach, which is about a 6-7 hour drive.
  2. Rest and relax for the day after your journey.

Day 2: Beach Activities in Nilaweli


  1. Enjoy a day at leisure on Nilaweli Beach. The beach is famous for its soft white sand and clear blue water, perfect for swimming and sunbathing.
  2. You can also enjoy water activities like snorkeling, diving, and windsurfing or just take a relaxing boat ride.
  3. In the evening, enjoy the sunset and the beautiful surroundings of the beach.

Day 3: Explore Trincomalee


  1. After breakfast, head to the nearby town of Trincomalee and explore the local markets and shops.
  2. Visit the historic Koneswaram Temple, located on a cliff overlooking the sea.
  3. Enjoy a fresh seafood lunch at a local restaurant.
  4. Visit the Dutch Bay Island, a small island with a beautiful beach and crystal-clear water.
  5. Return to your hotel in Nilaweli Beach in the evening.

Day 4: Pigeon Island National Park


  1. After breakfast, visit Pigeon Island National Park, one of the best places for snorkeling and diving in Sri Lanka.
  2. The island is home to a large number of coral species and marine animals, including reef sharks, sea turtles, and colorful fish.
  3. Spend the day exploring the island, snorkeling, and relaxing on the beach.
  4. Return to your hotel in the evening.

Day 5: Departure from Nilaweli


  1. After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer back to Colombo for your departure flight.

Yala National Park

Yala Tour Plan


$900.00

Wildlife and Adventure: A Yala National Park Tour


Day 1: Arrival in Colombo


  1. Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport in Colombo and transfer to your hotel near Yala National Park, which is about a 5-6 hour drive.
  2. Rest and relax for the day after your journey.

Day 2: Yala National Park


  1. After breakfast, visit Yala National Park for a full-day jeep safari.
  2. The park is known for its wildlife, including elephants, leopards, sloth bears, and a variety of bird species.
  3. Enjoy a picnic lunch in the park and continue the safari until evening.
  4. Return to your hotel in the evening.

Day 3: Bundala National Park and Kataragama


  1. After breakfast, visit Bundala National Park, which is famous for its birdlife, including migratory birds.
  2. After the park visit, proceed to the nearby town of Kataragama, a pilgrimage town that attracts devotees from different religions.
  3. Visit the Kataragama temple complex and participate in the evening pooja ceremony.
  4. Return to your hotel in the evening.

Day 4: Beach Activities in Tangalle


  1. After breakfast, head to the nearby town of Tangalle and spend the day at leisure on the beach.
  2. Tangalle beach is known for its pristine waters and golden sand, perfect for swimming and sunbathing.
  3. You can also enjoy water activities like snorkeling, diving, and surfing.
  4. Return to your hotel in the evening.

Day 5: Departure from Yala


  1. After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer back to Colombo for your departure flight.

Lipton Seat Tour Plan

Tea Country and Farm-to-Table Experience


Day 1: Arrival in Colombo


  1. Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport in Colombo and transfer to your hotel in Haputale, which is about a 5-6 hour drive.
  2. Rest and relax for the day after your journey.

Day 2: Lipton Seat and Dambetenna Tea Factory


  1. After breakfast, visit Lipton Seat, which is a viewpoint overlooking the tea estates of Haputale.
  2. Enjoy a scenic hike through the tea plantations to reach Lipton Seat.
  3. After enjoying the views, visit the nearby Dambetenna Tea Factory to learn about the tea-making process and sample some of the finest Ceylon tea.
  4. Return to your hotel in the evening.

Day 3: Haputale Agriculture Experience


  1. After breakfast, visit a local farm in Haputale to experience the traditional agricultural practices of Sri Lanka.
  2. Learn about the cultivation of fruits and vegetables that grow in the region, such as strawberries, carrots, and cabbage.
  3. Enjoy a lunch made from fresh produce grown on the farm.
  4. Return to your hotel in the evening.

Day 4: Tea Estate Tour and Factory Visit


  1. After breakfast, visit a tea estate in the area to learn about the history and culture of tea production in Sri Lanka.
  2. Observe the tea pluckers at work and learn about the different varieties of tea grown in the region.
  3. Enjoy a tea-tasting session to sample different flavors of Ceylon tea.
  4. Return to your hotel in the evening.

Day 5: Departure from Haputale


  1. After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer back to Colombo for your departure flight.
